Survey about Perception of MOOCs

Hi guys,

我是泡泡.I need your help with my course project. It will only take a couple minutes. Please help. This survey does not take any identification information and everything here is anonymous.

Survey questions are grouped for three important parties involved Massive open online courses (MOOCs): Student, Faculty, and Employer.

  • If you are a student, whether or not you are enrolled in MOOCs, please take survey questions A group https://goo.gl/forms/iNguDxILdtOInvkZ2;
  • If you are a faculty/teacher, whether or not you teach a MOOC, please take survey questions B group https://goo.gl/forms/etkPKYC1e7xTmys82;
  • If you are an HR person, recruiter, hiring manager, or anyone from an employer standing point, please take survey questions C group https://goo.gl/forms/zh9Z9XRJ8apCkkZF3.

Thank you very much for your precious time and valuable input.
